Emotion Analysis and Recognition based on Active Appearance Model
It is a system to detect human emotions such as happiness, sadness, neutral and surprise, the project was developed as a major project during my Graduation at ITM University. The system trains an Active Appearance Model and uses Inverse Compositional Project Out Algorithm as image alignment algorithm.
Although challenging, the project taught me many aspects of working within a team on a research project. The project will be taken further by publishing an indian database of facial images created during the project and writing a research and review paper regarding the same.
